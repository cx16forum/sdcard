Supports BMP, PCX, IFF (Amiga and PC variants), Koala (C64), Doodle (C64), and BMX (CX16).
Press a key to advance to the next image. 
Press Ctrl+C / RUN-STOP to stop color cycling in images that have them. After that, you can press a key to advance to the next image.
